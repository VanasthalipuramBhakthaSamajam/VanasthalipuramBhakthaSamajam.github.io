History & Aim

We want our traditions to continue for many generations so we have been conducting events such as gopooja and free gograsam for over 10 years in established gosalas near Vanasthalipuram. We help orphaned kids and people with disabilities by giving them donations based on their need for free. This generation of kids need to learn our traditions so we have been conducting events such as cultural dances, ancient arts of Hari Kadha & Burra Kadha, and essay writing competitions on important concepts to teach them our traditions and the importance of India. To further teach the latest telugu generation our traditions in a fun way, we have been performing and developing mythological(pauranika) poetic(padya) plays(natakam) to get more people to learn and take interest in these long forgotten plays. We have also been conducting eco-friendly events on auspicious days by planting some trees and distributing free plants to our neighborhood and any other people who like to participate in events like this. To engage our community in a way that suits best for our eyes, we also conduct tours to devotional and religious places (Punyakshetra) like Nepal, Sri Lanka, Gujarat, and much more.
